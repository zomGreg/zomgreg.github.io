517 N. Main Suite A
===================

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 2

Welcome to 517 N. Main Street in Spearfish SD. `Google Maps <https://goo.gl/maps/yC1x5xXuJTKKbyBP6>`_

Lease terms are for a minimum of 1 year (\$2000/mo), subleasing is permitted
with owner approval. Two dedicated off-street parking spaces, snow removal
included.

Space is approximately 1200 sf with two levels.
