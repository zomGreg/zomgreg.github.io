Who are you?

My name is Greg, I am a husband, brother, son, and an engineer for Enstratius, Inc. This site is not affiliated with or endorsed by Enstratius, although from time to time I may mention it.

I'm the smartest cloud guy you've never heard of, and this site is in part an effort to share some of the knowledge I've gained leading enterprise cloud projects around the world.
